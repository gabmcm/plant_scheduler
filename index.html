Welcome to Wuddr

Your easy to use water scheduling app.

To get started, input the last date that you watered your plants, an estimate of your indoor temperature, and how wet the soil is. The calculator will let you know the next date you should water your plants and how long to wait between each watering session. This is for a standard 10" pot, future pot sizes to be added at a later date.
